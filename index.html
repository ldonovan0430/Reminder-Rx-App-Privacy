Privacy Policy for Reminder Rx

Last updated: February 1, 2026

Introduction

Wheel Barrow Industries ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Reminder Rx mobile application. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Information Collection and Use

Reminder Rx stores your medication information, schedules, and reminders locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to backup via iCloud (managed by Apple's privacy policy).

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Your data is protected by iOS security features including device encryption and secure storage.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
