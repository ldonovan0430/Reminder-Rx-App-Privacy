Privacy Policy for Reminder Rx App

Last updated: February 1, 2026

Developer Information

Reminder Rx App is developed and maintained by Lauren Donovan. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Information Collection and Use

Reminder Rx App stores your medication information, schedules, and reminders locally on your device. The app does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to backup via iCloud, which is managed by Apple's privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

Data Security

Your data is protected by iOS security features including device encryption and secure storage. The developer does not have access to any data stored in the app.

User Rights

You maintain full control over your data. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or using the in-app settings.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
